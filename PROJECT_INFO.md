# Pay & Promise - Project Overview

## 1. Vision
To cultivate a global culture of integrity and personal growth where every commitment made is a promise kept. We envision a world where social connections and financial incentives work together to turn aspirations into achievements.

## 2. Mission
Our mission is to empower individuals to conquer procrastination and achieve their goals by combining high-stakes accountability with a premium, social experience. We provide the tools to make your word your bond.
